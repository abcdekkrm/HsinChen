Tasks Management Web App
ProTask is an online application where users are able to easily connect with each other to assign tasks and projects. The ProTask application sets itself apart from the pre-existing online management systems by offering novel functionality such as a busy factor to track business of connections, projects to better organize tasks and a public task board for users to pick up available tasks. Furthermore, there are user and task related skills to help managers understand the strengths and weaknesses of each of their connections, public connection boards to better aid users find other users with required skills and many other features.
